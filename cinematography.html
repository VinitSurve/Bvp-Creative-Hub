Cinematography Rules

Project Format 🎥
1. Accepted project formats: Video
2. Minimum video length: 30 seconds ⏳
3. Maximum video length: 10 minutes ⏰
4. Video file formats: MP4, MOV, MKV, AVI🎥
Project Content ✨
1. Cinematography projects must be original and not contain any copyrighted material. 🚫
2. Cinematography projects can showcase various genres, techniques, and styles. 🎞️
3. Cinematography projects should be visually appealing and demonstrate technical proficiency. 🎬
4. Cinematography projects should be free of watermarks, logos, or other branding. 🚫
5. Cinematography projects should not be sexually explicit or contain violent or disturbing content. 🔞
Additional Guidelines 🌟
1. You may upload a maximum of 5 cinematography projects per day. 🗓️
2. You may not upload the same cinematography project multiple times. 🔁
3. You may not upload cinematography projects that have been previously uploaded by another user. ❌
We review all cinematography projects before they are published. If a cinematography project does not meet our guidelines, we will remove it from the site. 🙅‍♀️🙅‍♂️
Thank you for your cooperation! 😊
