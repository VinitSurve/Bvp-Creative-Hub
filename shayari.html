Shayari Rules

Shayari Format ✍️

Accepted Shayari formats: Video, Audio, Written 🎥🎧📝
1. Minimum video length: 30 seconds ⏳
2. Maximum video length: 5 minutes ⏰
3. Audio file formats: MP3, WAV 🎶🎵
4. Written Shayari length: 2-20 lines 📃

Shayari Content ✨
1. Shayari must be original and not contain any copyrighted material. 🚫
2. Shayari can be in Hindi, Urdu, or any other language. 🗣️
3. Shayari should be free of watermarks, logos, or other branding. 🚫
4. Shayari should not be sexually explicit or contain violent or disturbing content. 🔞
Additional Guidelines 🌟
1. You may upload a maximum of 10 Shayari per day. 🗓️
2. You may not upload the same Shayari multiple times. 🔁
3. You may not upload Shayari that has been previously uploaded by another user. ❌
We review all Shayari before they are published. If a Shayari does not meet our guidelines, we will remove it from the site. 🙅‍♀️🙅‍♂️
Thank you for your cooperation! 😊
