Contact Format


📣 Attention all creatives! Please read all rules before submitting the Google Form 📣



🌟 Rules:
1. Read the rules and guidelines carefully on our website before submitting your work:
2. Fill out all required fields: Full Name, Email, College Branch, Course, Course Year, Talent Niche, Attach Files (max 5 files, total size 100MB), Instagram Profile Link.
3. Attach only files related to your talent.
4. Ensure the total size of all attached files does not exceed 100MB.

🤩 Required fields:
1. Full Name: Enter your full name as it appears on your official documents.
2. Email: Provide your valid email address where we can reach you regarding your submission.
3. College Branch: Select your current college branch.
4. Course: Specify your current course.
5. Course Year: Indicate your current academic year in the program.
6. Talent Niche: Choose the category that best represents your talent from the provided options.
7. Attach Files: Upload up to 5 files showcasing your talent, ensuring the total file size doesn't exceed 100mb. If your file is too large, use a tool on Google to make it smaller. We'll make it better once we have it.
8. Instagram Profile Link: Connect with us on social media by sharing your Instagram profile URL. 🌐 We're excited to give you the credits you deserve!🌟.
9. (Optional) Google Drive Link: 📁✨
If your files exceed the 100MB limit, please follow these steps: 👣
A) Upload your files to your Google Drive. ☁️✨
Create a folder if you have multiple files. 📂📁
B) Share the folder or file with "Anyone with the link can edit" permission. 🌐🧑‍💻
C) Paste the link in the "Google Drive Link" field. 🔗
Note: This option is only available for files that exceed the 100MB limit. 🚫🚫 If your files are under 100MB, please attach them directly to the form. 📎😊
🤩 Don't forget, all fields are mandatory to complete your submission. Once finished, click the "Submit" button to share your talent with the world! 🤩
