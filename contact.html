Contact Format


📣 Attention all creatives! 📣





For those submitting larger files (over 25 MB), please follow these steps to share your work with our team:
1. Sign in to your Google Drive account. ✨
2. For single files, choose the file you want to share and click "Open". 📂 For multiple files, create a new folder, add the files to the folder, and then right-click on the folder and select "Get link". 📁
5. Click "Copy link" to copy the link to your clipboard. 📋
6. Paste the copied link into the Google Drive link placeholder provided in the email. You'll find it within square brackets like this: [Your Google Drive link here]. Once you've inserted the link, your submission is complete! 🥳
7. When sharing the link, ensure you set the access permission to "Anyone with the link can edit." This will allow our team to make necessary edits and enhancements to your work. 🔒
8. Sit back and let your creativity shine! ✨
