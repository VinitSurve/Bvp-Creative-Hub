Drawing rules

File Types:-

1. Accepted file types: JPG, JPEG, PNG 🖼️
2. Minimum dimension: 1024px on either side
3. Maximum file size: 50MB ⚖️

Copyright:-

1. You must own the copyright to the drawings you upload. 🎨
2. If you are not the copyright owner, you must have permission from the copyright owner to upload the drawings. 📑
3. You must provide credit to the copyright owner in the description of the drawings. ✍️

Content:-

1. Drawings must be of high quality and relevant to the drawing niche. 🧑‍🎨
2. Drawings should be free of watermarks, logos, or other branding. 🚫
3. Drawings should not be sexually explicit or contain violent or disturbing content. 🔞

Additional Guidelines:-

1. You may upload a maximum of 10 drawings per day. 🗓️
2. You may not upload the same drawing multiple times. 🔁
3. You may not upload drawings that have been previously uploaded by another user. ❌
We review all drawings before they are published. If a drawing does not meet our guidelines, we will remove it from the site. ❌
Thank you for your cooperation! 😊
