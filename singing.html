Singing Rules

Performance Format 🎤

1. Accepted performance formats: Video, Audio 🎥🎧
2. Minimum video length: 30 seconds ⏳
3. Maximum video length: 5 minutes ⏰
4. Audio file formats: MP3, WAV 🎶🎵
5. Written format is not allowed 🚫
Performance Content 🎼 1. Singing performances must be original and not contain any copyrighted material. 🚫
2. Singing performances can be solo or group performances. 👯‍♀️👯‍♂️
3. Singing performances should be free of watermarks, logos, or other branding. 🚫
4. Singing performances should not be sexually explicit or contain violent or disturbing content. 🔞
Additional Guidelines 🎙️ 1. You may upload a maximum of 5 singing performances per day. 🗓️
2. You may not upload the same singing performance multiple times. 🔁
3. You may not upload singing performances that have been previously uploaded by another user. ❌
We review all singing performances before they are published. If a singing performance does not meet our guidelines, we will remove it from the account . 🙅‍♀️🙅‍♂️
Thank you for your cooperation! 😊
