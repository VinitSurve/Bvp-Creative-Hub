Mini Vlogs Rules

Video Format 🎬

Accepted video format: Video 🎥 1. Minimum video length: 30 seconds ⏳
2. Maximum video length: 2 minutes ⏰
3. Video file formats: MP4, MOV, MKV, AVI🎥
Video Content ✨
1. Mini vlogs must be original and not contain any copyrighted material. 🚫
2. Mini vlogs should be engaging and showcase a unique perspective or experience. 📹
3. Mini vlogs should be free of watermarks, logos, or other branding. 🚫
4. Mini vlogs should not be sexually explicit or contain violent or disturbing content. 🔞
Additional Guidelines 🌟
1. You may upload a maximum of 5 mini vlogs per day. 🗓️
2. You may not upload the same mini vlog multiple times. 🔁
3. You may not upload mini vlogs that have been previously uploaded by another user. ❌
We review all mini vlogs before they are published. If a mini vlog does not meet our guidelines, we will remove it from the site. 🙅‍♀️🙅‍♂️
Thank you for your cooperation! 😊
