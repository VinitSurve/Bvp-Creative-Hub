Poem Rules

Poem Format 📜

1. Accepted poem formats: Written, Audio, Video
2. Written poem length: 4-100 lines
3. Minimum video length: 30 seconds
4. Maximum video length: 5 minutes
5. Audio file formats: MP3, WAV
Poem Content 🎙️
1. Poems must be original and not contain any copyrighted material. 🚫
2. Poems can be in any language. 🗣️
3. Poems should be free of watermarks, logos, or other branding. 🚫
4. Poems should not be sexually explicit or contain violent or disturbing content. 🔞
Additional Guidelines 🎙️
1. You may upload a maximum of 10 poems per day. 🗓️
2. You may not upload the same poem multiple times. 🔁
3. You may not upload poems that have been previously uploaded by another user. ❌
We review all poems before they are published. If a poem does not meet our guidelines, we will remove it from the account. 🙅‍♀️🙅‍♂️
Thank you for your cooperation! 😊
