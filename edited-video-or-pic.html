Edited Video/Pic Rules

Content format🎬 * Accepted content formats: Video, Image 🎥🖼️
* Minimum video length: 30 seconds ⏳
* Maximum video length: 5 minutes ⏰
* Maximum image resolution: 4K (3840x2160) ✨
* Video file formats: MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV🎥
* Image file formats: JPEG, PNG 🖼️
**Content Guidelines** 🖼️
1. Edited videos or pictures must be original and not contain any copyrighted material. 🚫
2. Edited videos or pictures should be high quality and relevant to the niche. 🎨
3. Edited videos or pictures can be creative and engaging, showcasing editing skills and techniques. ✂️
4. Edited videos or pictures should be free of watermarks, logos, or other branding. 🚫
5. Edited videos or pictures should not be sexually explicit or contain violent or disturbing content. 🔞
**Additional Guidelines** ⚠️
1. You may upload a maximum of 5 edited videos or pictures per day. 🗓️
2. You may not upload the same edited video or picture multiple times. 🔁
3. You may not upload edited videos or pictures that have been previously uploaded by another user. ❌
We review all edited videos or pictures before they are published. If an edited video or picture does not meet our guidelines, we will remove it from account. 🙅‍♀️🙅‍♂️
Thank you for your cooperation! 😊
