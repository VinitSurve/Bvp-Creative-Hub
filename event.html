College Event Videos Rules

Video Format:-

1. Accepted video formats: MP4, MOV, AAV, MKV🎥
2. Minimum resolution: 720p 📺
3. Maximum video length: 10 minutes ⏳
4. Edited videos must be original and not contain any copyrighted material.

Video Content:-

1. Edited videos must be of high quality and relevant to the college event niche 💃🕺
2. Edited videos can be creative and engaging, showcasing the highlights of the event. 📹
3. Edited videos should be free of watermarks, logos, or other branding 🚫
4. Edited videos should not be sexually explicit or contain violent or disturbing content. 🔞

Additional Guidelines:-

1. You may upload a maximum of 5 edited videos per day. 🗓️
2. You may not upload the same edited video multiple times. 🔁
3. You may not upload edited videos that have been previously uploaded by another user. ❌
We review all edited videos before they are published. If an edited video does not meet our guidelines, we will remove it from our account🙅‍♀️🙅‍♂️.
