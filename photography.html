Photography rules

File Types:-

1. Accepted file types: JPG, JPEG, PNG 🖼️
2. Minimum dimension: 1024px on either side
3. Maximum file size: 100MB ⚖️

Copyright:-

1. You must own the copyright to the photos you upload. 📸
2. If you are not the copyright owner, you must have permission from the copyright owner to upload the photos. 📑
3. You must provide credit to the copyright owner in the description of the photos. ✍️

Content:-

1. Photos must be of high quality and relevant to the photography niche. 🌄
2. Photos should be free of watermarks, logos, or other branding. 🚫
3. Photos should not be sexually explicit or contain violent or disturbing content. 🔞

Additional Guidelines:-

1. You may upload a maximum of 10 photos per day. 🗓️
2. You may not upload the same photo multiple times. 🔁
3. You may not upload photos that have been previously uploaded by another user. 🚫
We review all photos before they are published. If a photo does not meet our guidelines, we will remove it from the account. ❌
Thank you for your cooperation! 😊
