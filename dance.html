Dance rules

Video Format:-

1. Accepted video formats: MP4, MOV 🎥
2. Minimum resolution: 720p 📺
3. Maximum video length: 10 minutes ⏳
4. Dance video must be original and not contain any copyrighted material. 🚫

Dance Content:-

1. Dance videos must be of high quality and relevant to the dance niche. 💃🕺
2. Dance videos can be solo or group performances. 👯‍♀️👯‍♂️
3. If the dance video features a group of dancers, please provide the Instagram IDs of all group members in the description. 📲
4. Dance videos should not be sexually explicit or contain violent or disturbing content. 🔞

Additional Guidelines:-

1. You may upload a maximum of 5 dance videos per day. 🗓️
2. You may not upload the same dance video multiple times. 🔁
3. You may not upload dance videos that have been previously uploaded by another user. ❌
We review all dance videos before they are published. If a dance video does not meet our guidelines, we will remove it from the site. 🙅‍♀️🙅‍♂️
Thank you for your cooperation! 😊
